About me

I am a final-year PhD student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), where I also received my B.Sc. in Computer Science in 2019.

I am interested in computational semantics, with a particular research focus of developing computational methods that would expand the intersection of theory-centric formal semantics and data-driven statistcal semantics. My PhD research studies sentence-level meaning representations through the lens of graph grammar and distributional semantics.

Feel free to reach me at aaronlo326 [at] gmail [dot] com!
