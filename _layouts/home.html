About me

I am a final-year PhD student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), where I also received my B.Sc. in Computer Science in 2019.

I study the use of computational methods for natural language, with a particular interest in computational semantics. My PhD research centres around sentence-level meaning representations, and studies them through the lens of graph grammars and distributional semantics.

Feel free to reach me at aaronlo326 [at] gmail [dot] com!
